# offsetreg: An Extension of 'Tidymodels' Supporting Offset Terms

Extend the 'tidymodels' ecosystem <https://www.tidymodels.org/> to
enable the creation of predictive models with offset terms. Models with
offsets are most useful when working with count data or when fitting an
adjustment model on top of an existing model with a prior expectation.
The former situation is common in insurance where data is often weighted
by exposures. The latter is common in life insurance where industry
mortality tables are often used as a starting point for setting
assumptions.
